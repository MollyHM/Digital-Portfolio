North Shore Banking App:

North Shore Banking is an app created to help encourage good banking habits, both for individuals who are just starting out with their first bank accounts, and more seasoned bankers who are trying to create better financial habits and experiences.

Problem Statement:

A significant portion of the population faces challenges when it comes to effectively managing their finances. This difficulty often translates into a lack of confidence and engagement when dealing with routine banking transactions. The primary objective behind developing this mobile banking app was to provide a solution for individuals who have previously had a less-than-ideal relationship with their finances.

Solution:

The goal was to create an environment where users could feel more comfortable and at ease when conducting their banking activities. To achieve this, we conducted an in-depth study of color psychology and effective composition techniques, all geared towards enhancing the user experience from the moment they log in to their banking app.

Research and Development:

I reviewed multiple bank platform for their user experience. I compared these experiences with online reviews to locate the most useful features and needed to be added or room to improve upon. This began the development process starting with hand sketching wireframe.

Key Features

Some key feature the app has to help users keep on track of their finances are:

  • Showing how much money has been spent from each account.
  • Easily accessed credit card infomation, such as credit limit, balance, and closing pay date

Testing Feedback

I received positive feedback that the app prioritizes the most common type of banking transactions. This helps the typical users easily navigate and achieve their banking goals. In contrast, I received feedback that squared shapes within the app should have rounded corners, as sharp edges are perceived as aggressive.

Results & Conclusion

The mobile banking app not only created an enjoyable banking experience for knowledgable banking users. It is also intuitive to those who are starting out with their first bank accounts and encouraging healthy banking habits.
